Places To Eat

There will be a buffet provided for all of our guests in the evening but if you are looking for somewhere to eat during the day here are a few suggestions:

Perkins (website)

Highly recommended restaurant in Keyworth. Great food, great wine, great service. Booking in advance is advised. 3 min drive, 20 min walk from the church. Parking available.

Keyworth Tavern (website)

Newly refurbished family-friendly pub/restaurant serving a range of bar meals. In Keyworth, 1 min drive, 5 min walk from the church. Parking available.

The Salutation Inn (website)

Another family friendly Keyworth pub serving lunch between 12pm and 2pm on a Saturday (though please check this with the pub). 3 min drive, 20 min walk from the church. Parking available.

Loughborough Restaurants

If you would prefer to eat nearer to Prestwold Hall, Loughborough town centre has many different options available: from cafes serving lighter options like Party Pieces and all the usual chain coffee shops, to flame-grilled chicken and steak houses like Fernandos. There are Italian restaurants (like Caravelli), Chinese, Indian and even Australian restaurants (like Moomba)! And of course there is a McDonald's, Subway and a Greggs (for all the Geordies!).
